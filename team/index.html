FL
Full Linkage Community Care Association
Independent Data Steward & s.88 Charity Partner
Hong Kong Section 88 registered charity
Institutional Partner

Full Linkage Community Care Association is a registered Hong Kong s.88 charity dedicated to community care. It is a separate legal entity with its own board, governance, and audited accounts — partnered with the HKU Swallowing Research Laboratory as co-stewards of the public clinical resource created by the CareEZ programme.

The co-steward structure is the data-governance answer to the most material objection an HKCSS or research-ethics reviewer can raise: who owns the clinical dataset, and what stops the applicant operator from privatising a public resource? Full Linkage's s.88 status anchors the public-interest disposition of the data. Consent forms, ethics protocols, retention rules, and downstream re-use terms are published under a governance framework co-developed by Full Linkage and HKU Swallowing Research Lab.

Carewells operates as the delivery vehicle; the clinical resource does not sit on Carewells' balance sheet, and its stewardship is constitutionally separated from any commercial product roadmap.
