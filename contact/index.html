About CareEZ

CareEZ is a clinical AI platform developed by our parent social enterprise, a Hong Kong social enterprise (SED-registered) founded to advance dysphagia management and texture-modified food safety for older adults and residential care home for the elderly (RCHE) operators. The platform combines multimodal AI swallowing assessment with real-time IDDSI-level classification, enabling care teams to identify aspiration risk, generate structured clinical reports, and match residents to appropriate texture-modified diets aligned with the HKCSS 護食標準 (Hong Kong Care-Food Standard).

Our parent social enterprise operates under a social enterprise model — reinvesting commercial revenue into subsidised access for smaller NGO-run care homes and community nutrition programmes. The company works with clinical advisors from Hong Kong's tertiary healthcare sector and holds partnership discussions with the Hong Kong Council of Social Service (HKCSS) and academic institutions including the University of Hong Kong (HKU). In addition to the CareEZ AI platform, our parent social enterprise produces care-food products under the SeniorDeli brand and maintains dysphagia.cn as a Mandarin clinical resource for the broader Greater China care sector.

For press enquiries, interviews, or asset requests, contact hello@careez.org or visit careez.org/press/ for the full press kit.
